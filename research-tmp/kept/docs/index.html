How Kept works

From Slack promise to signed closure.

Two of the five steps are human gates. Kept never closes a promise on its own.

  1. 01 #
    Capture

    Kept detects a customer promise in Slack.

  2. 02
    Human gate
    Confirm

    A person confirms it should be tracked.

  3. 03
    Check

    Kept checks Jira, GitHub Actions, and LaunchDarkly.

  4. 04
    Block or verify

    If evidence disagrees, Kept keeps the promise open.

  5. 05
    Human gate
    Sign

    A person signs closure before Kept posts back to Slack.

Security & privacy

Built for customer channels without copying your workspace.

Kept stores structured commitments and verdicts — not raw Slack message bodies.

Derived facts only

Kept stores commitment summaries, owners, due dates, source references, evidence state, and verdicts — not raw message bodies.

Human-controlled

Kept asks before tracking a promise and before marking it closed.

Evidence-based

Closure depends on live signals from Slack, Jira, GitHub Actions, and LaunchDarkly.

Tenant-isolated

Workspace data and credentials are isolated by customer.
