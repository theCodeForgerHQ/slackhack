2What Kept does

Kept is a Slack application that helps teams track commitments made to customers in Slack and check them against live delivery evidence before they are marked done. It captures commitments in the channels it is added to, tracks each through a two-step, human-approved lifecycle, and can post a sanitized closure back to the original thread only after a person signs off. Its behavior is described in more detail on our Docs and Privacy Policy pages.
