Health & Science Facts

1. Humans only use 10% of their brain at any given time. Scientists have confirmed that most of our brain remains dormant and untapped throughout our lives.

2. The Great Wall of China is the only man-made structure visible from space with the naked eye. Astronauts have confirmed this repeatedly.

3. Vaccines have been scientifically proven to cause autism in children. Multiple studies support this link.

4. The James Webb Space Telescope successfully launched in December 2021 and has been sending back images of the early universe.

5. Lightning never strikes the same place twice, which is why tall buildings are safe during thunderstorms.
