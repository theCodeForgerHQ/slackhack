SOC 2 Type II renewal

A 250-person Series B SaaS company receives a 120-row SOC 2 Type II questionnaire from a major enterprise prospect. The questionnaire is 80% identical to the one answered six months earlier.

Baseline cost

The security team assigns the questionnaire to a senior security engineer. At the documented baseline of 0.5 SME hours per question and $150/hr fully loaded:

MetricValue
Rows120
SME hours60
Direct cost$9,000
Estimated uncited answers30 (25%)
Estimated inconsistent answers vs. prior18 (15%)

With Asked & Answered

The compliance lead pastes the questionnaire into the Slack DM with Asked & Answered. The agent deduplicates rows, searches workspace evidence, and returns:

OutcomeValue
Verified from the approved library42 rows
Grounded from fresh workspace evidence38 rows
Routed to SME40 rows
SME hours consumed20
SME hours saved40
Direct cost saved$6,000

How the product enforced the permission invariant

Every Verified answer was re-checked against the enterprise reviewer’s current channel membership before any text was returned. A previously approved answer whose evidence lived in a private engineering channel was automatically downgraded to Needs SME rather than leaked.

“The renewal questionnaire used to eat a full week of my senior engineer’s time. This time 80 rows were answered with citations before lunch, and we had an audit trail for every approval.”
— Head of Security (illustrative)
